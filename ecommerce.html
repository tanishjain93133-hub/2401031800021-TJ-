Why Sustainable Fashion Matters

Sustainable fashion focuses on reducing environmental impact by using eco-friendly materials and ethical production practices. It helps reduce pollution and supports fair wages for workers.

Fast Fashion vs Sustainable Fashion

Fast fashion encourages overproduction and waste, while sustainable fashion promotes long-lasting, ethical, and environmentally responsible clothing choices.
